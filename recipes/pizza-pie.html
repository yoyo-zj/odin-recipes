Pizza Pie

A fine dish from Naples, Italy that has transformed the culinary world with its delicious flavors. I love pizza always have and always will. The rich taste and infinite customizability of pizza, across various toppings and preparations, easily confirm its status as an exceptional dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • .25 ounce active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2Â½ cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Sauce & Toppings of your choice

Steps

    Step 1
  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C), and lightly grease a pizza pan.
    2. Step 2
  2. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes
    3. Step 3
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth. You can do this by hand or use a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook to make it easier.
    4. Step 4
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes.
    5. Step 5
  5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.
    6. Step 6
  6. Transfer to the prepared pizza pan.
    7. Step 7
  7. Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice.
    8. Step 8
  8. Bake in the preheated oven unitl golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
